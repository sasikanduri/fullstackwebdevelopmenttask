DEALERS HELP

            
                     As the Dealer


                    - For new dealer to signup, there will be a signup form that ask for
                    following details - Name, Mobile Number, Nature of Material, Weight of
                    Material, Quantity, City and State.

                    -After user successful registration ,

                    - This user can book the drivers from the list of drivers in his dashboard whose
                    interested routes are the same as the dealer's location (State and City).
                    For eg: If the dealer’s location is Madhya Pradesh-Indore , all the drivers going
                    from/to Madhya Pradesh-Indore are displayed in the users dashboard

                    - users can also search Driver from (State and City) to (State and City).
